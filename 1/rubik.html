1-finger gestures

Swipe a row of cubies horizontally to rotate a cube face.
Swipe a column of cubies vertically to rotate a cube face.
This works on both 3D and 2D (unfolded) views.

2-finger gestures

Pinch and twist to rotate a face.
This works on both 3D and 2D views (ONLY SOME FACES IMPLEMENTED).

Keyboard

You may also use QWERTYASDFGH keys.
